Travel & Health Notices

General guidance for travellers. Always verify current rules with official health and border sources before departure.

EntryFAQInstitutions

Insurance

Travel medical insurance covering the Schengen area or Albania is strongly recommended. Some long-stay categories may require proof of coverage.

Public health

Requirements for vaccination certificates or testing may change according to WHO and national health policy. Consult the Ministry of Health and embassy notices close to your travel date.

Road safety & seasons

Mountain roads and winter conditions require caution. Coastal areas are busy in summer; book accommodation early.

Emergencies

Save local emergency numbers. EU citizens may use 112 where interoperable; Albania uses national emergency lines as published by authorities.
