Entry Requirements

Documents and conditions you should meet before travelling to Albania: passport validity, purpose of stay, proof of funds, and return ticket where applicable.

Travel document

Your passport or travel document must usually be valid beyond the intended stay. Some exemptions exist for bilateral agreements; check before departure.

Purpose of visit

You should be able to explain tourism, business, family visit, or other declared purpose. Carry invitations, hotel bookings, or itineraries matching your visa or visa-free entry.

Financial means

Border officers may ask for evidence that you can support yourself during your stay (cash, cards, sponsorship letter) in line with standard practice.

Return or onward travel

A confirmed return or onward ticket reduces questions at the border for short stays.
