Binding decisions

Only written decisions transmitted through official consular channels or the e-Visa system create rights or obligations. Informal guidance on third-party sites is not authoritative.

No guarantee of entry

A visa or exemption does not guarantee admission. The border authority verifies conditions at the time of crossing and may refuse entry on legal grounds.

Technical errors

While every effort is made to ensure accuracy of published fees and requirements, the administration reserves the right to correct manifest errors. Users should refresh official pages before payment.

Governing law

Disputes relating to the use of this portal and visa refusal procedures are governed by Albanian law and international treaties ratified by Albania, subject to jurisdiction rules.

Appeals

Appeals against visa refusal or annulment must be lodged within the statutory deadline with the reasons and evidence indicated in your notification, typically via the official email channel.
